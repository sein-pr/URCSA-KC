Description

The Christian Youth Ministry (CYM) nurtures faith and community among young believers. Guided by our motto, "Let CYM live and not die," we spread the Gospel through singing and preaching. We promote pride, loyalty, and respect, inspired by Philippians 4:13: “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.” Join us in growing in faith and building meaningful friendships!

Motto

Let CYM live and not die

Values

  • Faithfulness: We are committed to spreading the word of God through singing and preaching.
  • Pride: We take pride in our identity as members of CYM and our church community.
  • Loyalty: We demonstrate loyalty to CYM and its leadership.
  • Discipline: We practice diligence and self-discipline in our actions and commitments.
  • Respect: We treat one another with respect and kindness at all times.
  • Strength: We draw strength from our faith, inspired by Philippians 4:13, to overcome challenges together.

Vision

To empower young believers to live out their faith boldly and make a positive impact in our community.

Mission

To act as an ambassador for the URCSA Katutura in spreading the word of God in singing and preaching.
