Sunday School

Ministries > Sunday School

Description

The Sunday School is a children’s Ministry is a children's ministry aimed to nurture the spiritual, emotional, and social growth of children within a faith-based setting. These are the core purposes:

Spiritual Education and Development

The primary goal is to teach children about faith, biblical stories, and Christian values in an age-appropriate way. This helps children build a foundation for their relationship with God and grow in their understanding of their faith.

Moral and Ethical Guidance

Children’s ministry seeks to instill values such as kindness, honesty, and respect for others, helping children develop a strong moral compass. These teachings help shape their character and decision-making as they grow

Encouraging Active Participation

Children’s ministry often involves interactive activities like singing, crafts, drama, and games that engage children and make learning about faith enjoyable. These activities can also encourage children to participate in worship services and other church-related events.
