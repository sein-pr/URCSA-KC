Description

The Christian Men Ministry (CMM) empowers men to grow in faith and community. We promote leadership, service, and spiritual growth, inspired by Philippians 4:13: “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.” Join us in prayer, Bible study, and outreach as we strive to make a positive impact for Christ!

Motto

Empowering Men to Lead Through Faith and Service.

Values

  • Faith: Commitment to growing in our relationship with God.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and accountability in all actions.
  • Brotherhood: Fostering strong, supportive relationships among men.
  • Service: Dedication to serving our community and church.
  • Discipline: Pursuing diligence and commitment in our spiritual journey.
  • Respect: Valuing each other with kindness and honor.

Vision

To cultivate a community of men who lead with faith, integrity, and purpose, making a positive impact in their families and communities.

Mission

To empower men to grow spiritually, foster strong relationships, and serve our community through faith, leadership, and mutual support.
