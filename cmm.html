Description

The Christian Men Ministry (CMM) empowers men to grow in faith and community. We promote leadership, service, and spiritual growth, inspired by Philippians 4:13: “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.” Join us in prayer, Bible study, and outreach as we strive to make a positive impact for Christ!

Motto

Men in Service of Christ and His Church

Values

  • Faith in the Holy Trinity (Father, Son and the Holy Spirit)..
  • Patience and dedication to the service of God.
  • Humility and humbleness, in serving God and one another.
  • Love, kindness and care towards one another.
  • Truthfulness, trustworthiness toward one another
  • Respect for one self and one another.

Vision

To be a Christian Men`s Ministry that strives to the optimal realization of the current and future potential skills in the URCSA Christian Men Community in order to participate actively in the Mission of God to the World.

Mission

Worship, build the faith and the Spirituality of men so that they can be faithful witness for Christ in the Church and the Society, oppose which is in conflict with the Christian faith both within and outside the Church of Christ. Work in partnership with other Ministries within and with the Church in its ecumenical relations in building a community of believers where the walls of gender, race, language and culture are demolished and equality acknowledged

Objectives

  • To promote harmonization of the Church activities between Christian Men’s Ministry members and other Ministries
  • To promote high standard of professionalism in preaching and practising the true Gospelof Jesus Christ.
  • To make Jesus Christ known to all
  • To promote the stewardship among church members
  • To promote harmonious holy living between men and their families
  • To study God`s Word and enrich the lives of fellow Christians
  • To address the unique problems / challenges, needs and aspirations of the church community
