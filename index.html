Theme for 2024: “This, then, is how you should Pray…”

Prayer Bullets:

  • Lord, I acknowledge my sins and ask for forgiveness to destroy my selfishness and self-deception, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Father, forgive me for disobeying your laws and principles, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Blood of Jesus purify my heart from doubts, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Holy Spirit, help my unbelief and failure to live right, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Holy Spirit, guide me away from dishonesty and bad habits, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Holy Spirit, teach me to utilize my talent rightly for God’s work in Jesus’ Name.
  • Lord, destroy all spirit of procrastination and lukewarmness in my life, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Father, order my footsteps to go the way of your kingdom, in Jesus’ Name.
  • O’Lord put in my heart the spirit to be spiritually strong to fight and defeat my enemies, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Lord, give me the strength to jump over walls of difficulties in my life, in Jesus’ Name.
  • Angels of God go before me and arrest every spiritual evil hunter after my destiny.
  • O’Lord destroy every weapon of the devil fashioned against me, in Jesus’ Name. Amen.
