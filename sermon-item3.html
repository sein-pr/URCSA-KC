Sermon by: y Karlie Liebenberg, Others

Category: Purpose, God

Sep 22, 2019

Ref Karlie Liebenberg

Reads

  • Jeremiah 1:4-10

Full description

4 The word of the Lord came to me, saying, 5 “Before I formed you in the womb I knew you, before you were born I set you apart; I appointed you as a prophet to the nations.” 6 “Alas, Sovereign Lord,” I said, “I do not know how to speak; I am too young.” 7 But the Lord said to me, ... Read more

“Do not say, ‘I am too young.’ You must go to everyone I send you to and say whatever I command you. 8 Do not be afraid of them, for I am with you and will rescue you,” declares the Lord. 9 Then the Lord reached out his hand and touched my mouth and said to me, “I have put my words in your mouth. 10 See, today I appoint you over nations and kingdoms to uproot and tear down, to destroy and overthrow, to build and to plant.”

Thinks

  • God's Personal Knowledge: God knew Jeremiah even before he was formed in the womb. How does it feel to know that God knows us personally and has a plan for/with us?
  • Being Set Apart: Jeremiah was set apart and appointed by God as a prophet. What does it mean to be set apart by God, and how might that apply to our lives?
  • Overcoming Doubts: Jeremiah felt too young and unable to speak, but God assured him. How can we overcome self-doubt and trust God's plans for us?
  • Following God's Commands: God instructs Jeremiah to go where He sends him and speak as commanded. How can we be obedient to God's commands in our own lives?
  • God's Presence: God promises to be with Jeremiah and rescue him. How does the assurance of God's presence impact our fears and challenges?
  • Empowered by God: God touches Jeremiah's mouth and puts His words in it. How do God empower and guide us in our daily lives?

Listen

Questions

  • What excuses do you make for not being obedient to God?
  • What do you think the Lord is calling you for?

Prayers

  1. Prayer for Understanding Purpose

    Heavenly Father,
    Thank You for creating me with a purpose. Help me to understand Your plan for my life. Guide me in my journey, so I may fulfill the calling You have placed on my heart. May I seek Your will in all that I do. Amen.

  2. Prayer for Strength in Adversity

    Lord,
    In times of struggle and confusion, remind me that I am not alone. Just as You were with Jeremiah, be my strength and comfort. Help me to trust in Your power and to find hope in Your promises. Amen.

  3. Prayer for Justice and Trust

    Gracious God,
    When I face opposition or feel wronged, grant me the wisdom to trust in Your justice. Help me to release my desire for revenge and to find peace in knowing that You are in control. May I lean on Your strength to endure. Amen.

  4. Prayer for Healing and Comfort

    Loving Father,
    In moments of deep sorrow, I turn to You for comfort. Heal my heart and help me to express my pain to You. Remind me that You are close to the brokenhearted and that I can find solace in Your presence. Amen.

  5. Prayer for Empowerment

    Holy Spirit,
    Empower me to live out Your purpose in my life. Fill me with courage to share Your love and message with others. May I be a vessel of Your grace in a world that needs hope and healing. Amen.

  6. Prayer for Daily Guidance

    Almighty God,
    As I go through each day, guide my steps and decisions. Help me to align my actions with Your will and to remember that my life is a gift meant to glorify You. Teach me to live with intention and purpose. Amen.
