Repentance Classes Meeting Request

Repentance classes are open to all members who feel they have sinned and wish to repent. This includes:

  • Visible sins such as pregnancies out of wedlock.
  • Invisible sins such as theft, dishonesty, or any other spiritual transgressions.

If you feel the need for repentance and would like to schedule a meeting with the church administrator to evaluate your readiness for the classes, please fill out the form below.
