Application to Attend Confirmation Classes

Only those who have completed the catechesis course, or an equivalent approved Bible course, and have reached a specific age as prescribed by the church, will be allowed to confess their faith and be confirmed. The catechism classes extend over two years. Your application is to be submitted at the beginning of the first year.

Church Details

a) If you have not been baptised yet, it will take place at your confirmation.

b) It is the responsibility of prospective confirmands to submit a baptismal certificate before the end of the first year.

c) If you were baptised outside the congregation, ask the minister/secretary of the church council, as soon as possible, to request your baptismal certificate from the congregation/church where you were baptised in order for you to provide the abovementioned particulars.

Personal resolution

In the period of catechesis, the following will be expected of you:

a) To attend catechism lessons regularly.

b) To attend the church services regularly.

c) To belong to or participate in at least one of the organizations or activities of the congregation (e.g.: Youth movement/CYM, Women's movement/CWM, Congregation Choir, Men movement/CMM, etc...).

d) To participate in the weekend camps, church bazaars, and other activities organized.

e) To diligently do the assignments, which are given to you.

f) To do Bible study and to pray regularly.

g) To lead a sober and exemplary life. The congregation, the Church council and, in particular, the elder will supervise your conduct and progress.
