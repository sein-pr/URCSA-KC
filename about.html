The Church's Hierarchy Structure

The hierarchy is structured as follows:

  1. One General Synod Commission (S.A)
  2. Seven Regional Synod Commissions (6 in S.A, 1 in Namibia)
  3. Presbyteries Commissions (various in S.A; 5 in Namibia)
  4. Congregations (about 24 in Namibia)
  5. Church Council (governing body of each Congregation)
  6. Church members (the core of the Churchâ€™s existence)

Note: Congregations can establish Wards that may become independent if self-sustaining.

Greatest Treasures of the Church

Our church is proud to be home to one of the first original Bibles, a treasured artifact that represents our deep-rooted faith and history. We invite you to come and experience this remarkable piece of our spiritual heritage, which has inspired countless lives throughout the years.
